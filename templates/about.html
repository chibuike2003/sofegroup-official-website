About Sofe Group

Driving Digital Education and Blockchain Adoption Across Africa.

Our Mission

To build Africa’s future by equipping individuals with the knowledge and resources needed to thrive in the evolving tech landscape, focusing on digital education, blockchain adoption, and tech literacy.

Our Impact

  • Empowered Over 5,000 Individuals: Trained in essential Web3 and digital skills through virtual and in-person learning.
  • Developed Tech Professionals: Mentored thousands in blockchain, cryptocurrency, and emerging technologies, now contributing to the ecosystem.
  • High-Impact Initiatives: Organized bootcamps, training programs, and community-building efforts.
  • Strategic Partnerships: Collaborated with leading blockchain firms for free and accessible educational resources.
  • Broad Reach: Engaging universities, institutions, and tech communities across Africa.

Our Approach

  • Education as Key: Structured and practical training in blockchain and digital skills.
  • Community Engagement: Hosting physical meetups, IRL events, and hackathons.
  • Recognized Learning: Certificates upon completion for career and professional development.
  • Career Advancement: Providing mentorship and career support.

Our Recent Initiatives

  • Blockchain Career Expo: Scaling Africa’s tech manpower by connecting talent and stakeholders.
  • Web3 Developer Bootcamps: Intensive training in Solidity, Rust, and blockchain development.
  • University Partnerships: Introducing blockchain education across Nigerian and African universities.
  • Community-Led Learning: Dedicated groups for DeFi, trading, and blockchain development.
  • Industry Partnerships: Collaborating for learning and job placements.

Why We Do This

Driven by the vision of a digitally empowered Africa, leveraging technology for financial inclusion and economic growth. We believe in bridging the digital divide through education, mentorship, and collaboration.
